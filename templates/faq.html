Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Sky Baby Store.

Orders & Shopping

Browse our product catalogue, click Add to Cart on any item you like, then go to your Cart and click Proceed to Checkout. Review your order summary, choose a payment method, and click Place Order. You will receive a confirmation once your order is registered.

You can browse all products without an account. However, to add items to the cart and place an order, you need to create a free account or log in. Registration takes less than a minute.

Once an order has been placed, please contact us immediately via phone or email and we will do our best to accommodate changes before the order is dispatched. After dispatch, changes may not be possible. Reach us at shoproy151@gmail.com or +256 756 762 813.

Go to your Cart and use the + and − buttons next to each item to adjust the quantity. To remove an item completely, click the trash icon or reduce the quantity to zero. You can also clear the entire cart using the Empty Cart button.
Payment

We accept MTN Mobile Money, Visa / Mastercard, and Cash on Delivery. All transactions are handled securely.

Delivery

We currently deliver across Kampala and surrounding areas in Uganda. For deliveries outside Kampala, please contact us directly to arrange shipping.

Orders within Kampala are typically delivered within 1–3 business days. You will be contacted to confirm your delivery slot.

Yes — delivery is currently free for all orders within our covered delivery zones. We will notify you if delivery charges apply for your area.
Products & Returns

Absolutely. All products stocked at Sky Baby Store are carefully selected from trusted suppliers and meet applicable safety standards for infant and toddler use.

We accept returns or exchanges within 7 days of delivery for items that are unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase. Please contact us to initiate a return.

Out-of-stock items are clearly labelled on the product page. You can contact us to check restock dates or request to be notified when the product becomes available again.
